About Us

Marketyaab is a dynamic Digital Marketing Agency established in 2023, dedicated
to pushing startups and entrepreneurs towards sky rocketed growth.

We Are Marketyaab

Our name, derived from the Afghan language, combines "Market," representing the epicenter of commerce, with "Yaab," meaning finder in Dari, signifying our role in discovering markets for businesses.

We exist because we realized something simple: lots of businesses need help with marketing, but they can't afford to hire full-time staff or expensive freelancers. Meanwhile, there are talented people in Afghanistan who want to work but don't have many opportunities, so, we decided to bring them together. MarketYaab connects these businesses with skilled freelancers in Afghanistan who offer great services at affordable prices. This helps businesses grow and gives talented people the chance to work on cool projects. But we're not just about making money. We also want to change how people think about work. We believe that freelance work can help countries like Afghanistan grow and become more modern.

Mission

At MarketYaab, we have two big goals: to help businesses with marketing and to give freelancers cool projects to work on. We think freelance work is a great way to help countries grow and change for the better. That's why we're working hard to make it easier for businesses and freelancers to connect.

Vision

We dream of a world where anyone can find great work, no matter where they live. At MarketYaab, we're not just building a business; we're building a community. We want to help businesses grow and give freelancers the chance to shine.
